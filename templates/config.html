This web page dynamically generates the Coxeter projection of a representation of a simply-laced Lie algebra, which is a projection from a higher dimensional weight space onto a complex plane.

Here we consider a representation of \(\mathfrak{g} = A_n, D_n, E_6\), or \(E_7\) whose highest weight \(v_0\) is a fundamental weight \(\omega_i\), and display the projections of the weights in the representation and the roots connecting the weights.

Physically, a Coxeter projection encodes the BPS spectrum of a 2d \(\mathcal{N} = (2,2)\) Landau-Ginzburg model. For more detail, see

  • Polytopes and solitons in integrable, \(\mathcal{N}=2\) supersymmetric Landau-Ginzburg theories, [Lerche-Warner (1991)]
  • 2d SCFTs from M2-branes, [Hori-Park-Tachikawa (2013)]
  • 2d SCFT from M-branes and its spectral network, [Park (2014)]

Examples

  • \(\mathfrak{g} = A_2, v_0 = \omega_1\) (Solitons of \(G/H = \mathrm{SU}(3)/[\mathrm{SU}(2) \times \mathrm{U}(1) ]\))
  • \(\mathfrak{g} = A_3, v_0 = \omega_1\) (Solitons of \(G/H = \mathrm{SU}(4)/[\mathrm{SU}(3) \times \mathrm{U}(1) ]\))
  • \(\mathfrak{g} = A_3, v_0 = \omega_2\) (Solitons of \(G/H = \mathrm{SU}(4)/[\mathrm{SU}(2) \times \mathrm{SU}(2) \times \mathrm{U}(1) ]\))
  • \(\mathfrak{g} = D_4, v_0 = \omega_1\) (Solitons of \(G/H = \mathrm{SO}(8)/[\mathrm{SO}(6) \times \mathrm{SO}(2)]\))
  • \(\mathfrak{g} = E_6, v_0 = \omega_1\) (Solitons of \(G/H = {\mathrm{E}_6}/[\mathrm{SO}(10) \times \mathrm{U}(1)]\))
