This web page dynamically generates the Coxeter projection of the weights of a representation of a simply-laced Lie algebra, which is a projection of the representaion from its higher dimensional weight space to a complex plane.

A Coxeter projection encodes the BPS spectrum of a 2d \(\mathcal{N} = (2,2)\) Landau-Ginzburg model. For more detail, see

  • Polytopes and solitons in integrable, \(\mathcal{N}=2\) supersymmetric Landau-Ginzburg theories, [Lerche-Warner (1991)]
  • 2d SCFTs from M2-branes, [Hori-Park-Tachikawa (2013)]
  • 2d SCFT from M-branes and its spectral network, [Park (2014)]
